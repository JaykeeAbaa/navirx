About NaviRx

NaviRx, LLC partners with organizations across the prescription landscape to improve patient access and affordability while streamlining workflows and technology. Our expertise spans pharmacy operations, healthcare technology, and the standards that connect them—helping clients navigate strategy, integrations, and scalable execution.

Miranda
Founder & Principal Consultant

Miranda brings over 25 years of healthcare experience and more than 15 years in health tech leadership. She has been deeply involved in electronic prescribing since the early 2000s, shaping industry standards, driving system integrations, and optimizing workflows across the healthcare ecosystem.

Read full bio

Her work has spanned providers, EHRs, intermediaries/switches, pharmacy systems, and pharmacies, giving her a 360-degree view of prescription access and technology.

In the early 2000s, Miranda earned her PTCB certification and worked as a Pharmacy Technician in both hospital in-patient and retail pharmacy settings, later training others entering the profession. Around that same time, she became involved in electronic prescribing, helping to shape industry standards, system integrations, and workflows across providers, EHRs, intermediaries/switches, pharmacy systems, and pharmacies.

In the early 2010s, she helped launch three Accountable Care Organizations (ACOs) and has consistently explored innovative reimbursement strategies to improve patient access and affordability.

Miranda has advised pharmaceutical manufacturers, digital health platforms, pharmacy technology vendors, and independent pharmacies on solutions that expand patient access, optimize pricing and reimbursement, and align with industry standards such as NCPDP SCRIPT and RTPB. Her approach emphasizes practical workflows, scalable technology, and measurable impact — helping organizations navigate the complex intersection of prescription technology, affordability, and innovation.
